# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**SUPER_ADMIN** — Internal staff who manage the entire system: create users, assign team members to clients, configure forms, view all submissions and files, manage settings.

**TEAM_MEMBER** — Account managers / support staff assigned to specific clients. They view client submissions, post comments (internal + external), upload files, and manage their assigned client relationships.

**CLIENT** — External users who submit forms, upload files, and communicate with their assigned team members. They only see their own data.

## Product Purpose

ClientConnect is a secure client communication portal. It replaces email threads and shared drives with a structured system where clients submit intake forms, upload files, and communicate with a service team — all in one place, with audit trails. Success means clients get faster responses, staff have clear workflows, and nothing falls through the cracks.

## Brand Personality

Professional, trustworthy, direct. Not flashy — the interface earns confidence through clarity and reliability, not decoration. Blue as an accent signals security without shouting.

Three words: **Clear. Reliable. Respectful.**

## Anti-references

- Overly complex SaaS dashboards with dense navigation and visual noise. Every screen should make the primary action obvious.
- "Friendly startup" interfaces with playful illustrations and casual copy. ClientConnect needs to feel like a secure business tool, not a consumer app.
- Generic AI-generated designs: gradient text, glassmorphism, side-stripe borders, SaaS-cliché hero metrics. None of that belongs here.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity first.** Every screen has one primary task. Labels are plain language. Errors tell you what to fix, not just what went wrong.
2. **Progressive disclosure.** Show the most important information immediately; nest secondary details behind expand/collapse or detail views. Don't overwhelm with data.
3. **Trust through consistency.** The same patterns (tables, forms, badges, spacing) across all three roles so staff and clients feel equally at home.
4. **Security without friction.** Auth is clean and fast. Rate limiting, session expiry, and access controls are invisible until they need to act.
5. **On brand at rest.** The brand is an accent, not a wallpaper. Blue appears in focused places (active nav, primary buttons, links) — the rest is neutral and stays out of the way.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Body text contrast ≥4.5:1. All interactive elements focus-visible. Reduced motion respected throughout. The auth flow has clear error announcements for screen readers.
